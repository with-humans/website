With humans is a consultancy, organised as a collective of people.
Picture of people/collective?
We create software. With you, for you, with humans, for humans. We do this, while employing modern and up-to-date development practices together with incremental and lean processes. This way we ensure to build the right thing. And to build it right.
We act as mentors, coaches and trainers to teams. Either by way of embedding ourselves into teams fully, supporting you in your day-to-day work. Sometimes we might do this for longer periods of time.
Picture of people/collective?
Other times we might mentor and coach individuals and organisations in improving their effectiveness developing software and leading people.
Sometimes we can best support an organisation by designing and giving workshops and trainings tailored to that organisation's needs.
At the same time with humans is a wider network of people, consisting of more than the collective's workers, who all care about and support each other.
